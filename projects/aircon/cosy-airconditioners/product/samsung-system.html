Self-Diagnosis Mode

The unit's self-diagnosis mode alerts users to malfunctions straight away. An error code and LED light enable engineers to identify the problem quickly, reducing the time taken to fix it.

When indoor space is at a premium, the 2 Way Cassette is an ideal solution, particularly in long narrow areas such as hallways and corridors. As it is 45 per cent smaller than previous units, and requiring 30 per cent less room than the 4 Way cassette, it is easily incorporated into existing or planned building designs. Conveniently slotting into standard ceiling grids, the result is a discreet air conditioning system which offers maximum performance with minimum visual impact on decor.
